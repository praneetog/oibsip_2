About Lionel Messi

Club Play

Lionel Messi, widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, has enjoyed illustrious careers with two clubs: Barcelona and Paris Saint-Germain (PSG). During his tenure at Barcelona, Messi amassed numerous achievements, including 10 La Liga titles, 7 Copa del Rey titles, and 4 UEFA Champions League titles. He also secured a record-breaking 7 Ballon d'Or awards. In 2021, Messi transferred to PSG, where he continued to exhibit his exceptional talent, contributing to the team's pursuit of domestic and continental success. He currently plays for Inter miami.

International Career

Despite Messi's amazing club career, he was considered a flop with his national team. He didn't win a single trophy with his national team despite his good performance. He lost a World Cup final in 2014, Copa América finals in 2015 and 2016. After a third-place finish at the 2019 Copa América, Messi led Argentina to victory in the tournament two years later, and he received the Golden Ball award. He also won 2022 Finalissima in London. His success continued at the 2022 World Cup. There he guided Argentina to the finals, where he scored two goals to help defeat France. Messi won the World Cup's Golden Ball, becoming the first male player to receive that award twice. In addition, his outstanding play in the tournament was instrumental in Messi winning his eighth Ballon d'Or in 2023.
